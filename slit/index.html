What is this and why are you doing it?

According to a recent survey we are sepctacularly bad at having basic ballpark figures in our society.

It is our assertion that the more accurately people understand what reality looks like, the more empowered they are to contribute constructively to political debate.

Accordingly, SL aims to help people make sure that their perceptions of our society are aligned with the best available information.
